Important Information

What to do if you think you have COVID-19

Self Isolate

Isolate yourself from others for at least 14 days to prevent further spread

Talk to a HealthBC nurse

Call 8-1-1 to talk to a HealthBC nurse. They can provide guidance on your next steps

Call 9-1-1

If symptoms get worse, call 9-1-1 immediately

BC is currently in Phase 1 of the Restart Plan

Only essential services are currently operating, including:

  • Essential health services
  • Food production and distribution
  • Other designated critical infrastructure services.

Physical distancing measures are still currently in place. These include:

  • Keeping 2m apart from others
  • Minimize outdoor travel
  • Stay at home if you feel sick.

For a comprehensive list of open services, please visit the BC CDC website.
